Brand introduction


Nai Xue tea is a well-known tea brand in China. Founded in 2015, nai Xue tea is headquartered in Shenzhen, Guangdong Province and belongs to Shenzhen Pindao Catering Management Co., LTD. It innovates the form of "tea + soft Euro bag", with young women aged 20-35 as the main customer group. It insists on changing the tea base every 4 hours, and the soft Euro bag does not stay overnight.

Nai Xue tea insists on providing modern people with a good life experience of "a cup of good tea, a soft euro bag", and hopes to become the innovator and promoter of Chinese tea culture to the world.

In December 2017, Nai Xue tea began to expand beyond Guangdong to the whole country, officially launching the "National Urban Expansion Plan". As of December 2020, Nai Xue tea has spread across 70 cities and nearly 500 stores in China. All stores are directly operated and do not join in any form. In March 2018, Nai Xue completed A+ round of investment and reached an valuation of 6 billion yuan, becoming the first unicorn in the tea industry in public information.
